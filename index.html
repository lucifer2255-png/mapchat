⚡ SWOT Analysis
💪 Strengths
Real-time GPS precision with sub-50m accuracy
Zero friction onboarding — no account needed to join rooms
First-mover in hyper-local ephemeral chat space in India
⚠️ Weaknesses
Battery drain from continuous GPS tracking
Privacy concerns around location sharing
Cold start problem in low-density areas
🌱 Opportunities
India's 500M+ smartphone users, event economy growing 18% YoY
College fest, IPL, concerts — massive untapped TAM
B2B play: venue and event management integrations
🚨 Threats
Meta/Google launching similar features in WhatsApp/Maps
Data privacy regulation (DPDP Act 2023)
Low retention without strong content network effects
