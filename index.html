Event organisers lack real-time, location-aware tools to foster crowd energy or deliver updates to their precise audience.

⚖️ SWOT Analysis
💪 Strengths
Unique geofencing-first UX with no friend-request friction
Ephemeral privacy by design — auto-deletion after 24h
Clear B2B analytics revenue stream from day one
Strong Gen Z cultural fit with IRL trend alignment
⚠️ Weaknesses
High CAC in event-dense cities due to geo-targeting
Dependence on GPS accuracy and 5G availability
Network effect bootstrapping challenge at launch
Limited brand recognition vs established platforms
🌱 Opportunities
$30B LBS market projected by 2026 — wide open space
IRL social trend accelerating post-COVID among Gen Z
Eventbrite / Ticketmaster API integration potential
University campus virality loops for organic growth
🔴 Threats
Big Tech (Meta, Google) could add geo-chat features
User privacy regulation — GDPR, DPDP Act compliance
Low switching costs for users between platforms
Copycat risk post-launch without strong patent moat
